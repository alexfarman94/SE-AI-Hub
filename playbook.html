The SE AI Hub Playbook

Your strategic guide to leveraging AI at every stage of the sales cycle to save time, deliver more value, and win more deals.

New to the Hub? Start Here.

This playbook is the best place to start. It connects our AI tools and prompts directly to your daily workflow, showing you exactly how and when to use them for maximum impact.

RFPs & RFIs

Prospecting

Efficiently qualify and respond to inbound requests for information and proposals.

Recommended Assistants:

  • RFP Helper: Get fast, consistent answers to RFP questions.
  • Go/No-Go GPT (Coming Soon): Analyze inbound opportunities against key criteria.

Pre-Call Strategy & Research

Discovery

Walk into every discovery call with a deep understanding of the prospect's business, stakeholders, and likely challenges.

Recommended Assistants:

  • Deal Prep: Your first stop for any new opportunity. Get a complete, automated brief by connecting to Salesforce and Gong.

Key Prompts from the Library:

  • Company & Persona Research Brief
  • LinkedIn Profile Stakeholder Brief
  • Brainstorm Discovery Questions
3

Discovery & Solutioning

Discovery & Evaluating

Map the prospect's pains directly to Bob's capabilities and build the foundation for a winning demo.

Recommended Assistants:

  • Deal IQ: Get an unbiased, critical evaluation of deal health directly from the call transcript.
  • M2 Metrics Question Master: Generate metric-focused questions to help build a strong business case.

Key Prompts from the Library:

  • Pain-to-Solution Mapping
  • "Day in the Life" Demo Narrative
  • Custom Demo Data Generator
4

Demo & Proof

Evaluating & Proof

Deliver a tailored, value-driven demo and handle any technical or competitive questions with confidence.

Recommended Assistants:

  • Deal Prep: Re-engage and catch up on context before a big proof-of-concept call.
  • UK Payroll SME / IAM Integrations SME: Get fast, accurate answers to deep technical questions on the fly.
  • Integration Architect: Dynamically create client-facing technical architecture diagrams.

Key Prompts from the Library:

  • Tailored Demo Script Creator
  • Competitive Battlecard Creator
  • Objection Handling Matrix
  • Integration Value Proposition

Post-Demo & Closing

Evaluating & Proof

Maintain momentum, enable your champion, and ensure a smooth transition to post-sales teams.

  • Deal Handover Hub (Coming Soon): Automate the administrative work of deal transition.

Key Prompts from the Library:

  • Draft Demo Follow-Up Email
  • Champion Enablement Email (Power Play)

Deal Reviews & QBRs

Evaluating & Proof

Provide data-driven insights for internal deal reviews and Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs).
