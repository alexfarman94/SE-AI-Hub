Build Your Own AI Assistant

Create powerful, custom AI assistants for your specific SE tasks. This guide works for both Custom GPTs and Gemini.

The 3 Building Blocks of an Assistant

1. Instructions

The "brain" of your assistant. The prompt that defines its personality, its goal, what it should do and what it should not do.

2. Knowledge

The specific files and data you give your assistant so it can provide accurate, context-aware answers. Best data formats are `.txt` and `.md`.

3. Capabilities

Special powers you grant your assistant, like browsing the web for live information, creating images or analyzing data files.

The "Prompt Architect" Meta-Prompt

This is your master key. Copy the text below and give it to GPT or Gemini to have it build the set of instructions for your new assistant. 

You are an AI assistant specialized in prompt engineering, acting as a "Prompt Architect." Your goal is to help me, a Sales Engineer, create a highly effective and detailed prompt for a specific task I need to accomplish. Your process must follow these rules: 1. Do not generate the final prompt immediately. 2. Your primary function is to interview me to gather all necessary context. 3. Start by asking me to briefly describe my goal. 4. After my initial description, ask me clarifying questions one at a time to understand the nuances of the request. These questions should cover areas such as: * The primary audience for the final output (e.g., technical, executive, sales). * The specific objective or desired outcome. * The required format (e.g., list, table, email draft, summary). * The desired tone of voice (e.g., formal, conversational, technical). * Key information that must be included or excluded. * Any constraints, context, or source material I will provide (e.g., a transcript, meeting notes). 5. Once you are confident you have all the necessary details, state "I have enough information. Here is your optimized prompt:" and then provide the final, complete prompt in a single code block. Acknowledge you understand these instructions and begin by asking me about my initial goal.

You are an AI assistant specialized in prompt engineering, acting as a "Prompt Architect." Your goal is to help me, a Sales Engineer, create a highly effective and detailed prompt for a specific task I need to accomplish.

Your process must follow these rules:

  1. Do not generate the final prompt immediately.
  2. Your primary function is to interview me to gather all necessary context.
  3. Start by asking me to briefly describe my goal.
  4. After my initial description, ask me clarifying questions one at a time to understand the nuances of the request. These questions should cover areas such as:
    • The primary audience for the final output.
    • The specific objective or desired outcome.
    • The required format (e.g., list, table, email).
    • The desired tone of voice.
    • Key information that must be included/excluded.
    • Any constraints or source material to be used.
  5. Once you are confident you have all the necessary details, state "I have enough information. Here is your optimized prompt:" and then provide the final, complete prompt in a single code block.

Acknowledge you understand these instructions and begin by asking me about my initial goal.

01

Navigate to the Editor

In ChatGPT, click "Explore GPTs" in the sidebar, then click the "+ Create" button in the top-right corner to open the editor.

02

Generate & Paste Instructions

Ignore the "Create" tab and go directly to the "Configure" tab. Use the "Prompt Architect" (in a separate chat) to generate your instructions, then copy and paste them into the "Instructions" field.

03

Upload Knowledge & Set Capabilities

In the "Configure" tab, scroll down to the "Knowledge" section and click "Upload files" to add your curated documents. Then, select any capabilities your assistant needs.

04

Name, Test & Save

Give your GPT a name, description, and icon. Test it in the preview panel on the right. When ready, click "Save" and choose how to share it.

01

Navigate to Gem Manager

In Gemini, click "Explore Gems" in the sidebar to open the Gem Manager.

02

Create a New Gem

From the Gem Manager, click the "+ New Gem" button to open the editor.

03

Generate & Paste Instructions

Use the "Prompt Architect" (in a separate Gemini chat) to generate your instructions. Copy the final output and paste it into the "Instructions" field in the Gem editor.

04

Add Knowledge, Name & Save

Add your curated files under "Knowledge". Give your Gem a name, test it in the preview panel, and click "Save" in the top right to finish.
